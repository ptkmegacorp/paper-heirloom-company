METHODOLOGY & DISCLAIMERS
================================================================================

Volume (Vol): Estimated US monthly searches — tiered, not pulled from a live tool.
  VH  = very high   10,000+
  H   = high        1,000 – 9,999
  M   = medium      100 – 999
  L   = low         10 – 99
  VL  = very low    1 – 9
  —   = unknown / programmatic long-tail (build for coverage, validate later)

Difficulty (KD): Estimated ranking difficulty 1–100 (higher = harder).
  Low      1–25   realistic for new domain with strong page + backlinks over time
  Medium   26–50  needs authority, content depth, or long-tail angle
  High     51–75  competitive SERPs (gift roundups, Etsy, Pinterest, major blogs)
  Very High 76–100 broad head terms; deprioritize early

Intent:
  C  = commercial (ready to buy)
  T  = transactional (product search, Etsy)
  I  = informational (how-to, ideas)
  N  = navigational (brand)
  M  = mixed

Priority (P):
  P1 = ship first (best product fit + winnable)
  P2 = wave 2 hubs and high-intent spokes
  P3 = programmatic scale / long-tail
  P4 = monitor only until domain authority grows

Page status:
  LIVE     = exists today
  PLANNED  = assigned URL, not built
  PROG     = programmatic template (many variants from one pattern)

Site base (current): /
Future custom domain example: paperheirloomcompany.com/{slug}/
