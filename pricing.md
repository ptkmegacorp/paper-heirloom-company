**Status:** Draft

## Pricing strategy

We price **~10% above comparable Etsy listings** for similar bridal shower gifts, origami place card sets, and fold-together wedding table details.

That premium reflects:

- Flat printed sheets with fold guides (not generic blank paper)
- Shower-to-table story — one product, two moments
- Curated presentation and gift-ready packaging
- Paper Heirloom Company brand positioning (keepsake, not disposable craft)

Before listing, benchmark 3–5 Etsy comps on:

- origami bridal shower gift / activity sets  
- wedding place card holders (DIY or flat-pack)  
- group shower gifts with guest-count pricing  

**Rule:** Our base price ≥ comp average × **1.10** (round to sensible Etsy price points, e.g. $49 / $59 / $69).

## Color tiers

### Standard — white, lavender, green

Default line at our **base price point**. Same product, same fold, same shower → wedding journey.

| Color   | Role        | Notes                                      |
|---------|-------------|--------------------------------------------|
| White   | Standard    | Clean, classic; broadest appeal            |
| Lavender| Standard    | Soft, bridal; fits garden/spring showers   |
| Green   | Standard    | Botanical; pairs with garden wedding vibe  |

- One price tier for all three  
- Guest-count sizing (e.g. 25 / 50 / 75 / 100) scales within this tier  
- Position as the accessible entry to Paper Heirloom  

### Premium upsell — black

**Black is the upsell.** Matte black paper with gold accents (and optional white letterpress / print detail) sits **above** standard colors.

| Color | Role    | Notes                                                |
|-------|---------|------------------------------------------------------|
| Black | Premium | Formal, sculptural; highest perceived value          |

- **Higher price** than white / lavender / green at every guest-count tier  
- Market as limited / elevated / “Dark Swan” aesthetic  
- Ideal for formal weddings, evening receptions, Japanese-inspired tables  
- Shower gift edition and solo fold-at-home sets can both use this tier  

**Upsell logic:** Lead with standard colors on Etsy and site; offer black as “upgrade” in listing variants or a separate premium listing.
